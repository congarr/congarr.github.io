>watson district

The Watson District is one of the six districts of Night City in Cyberpunk 2077. Being the district where you start the game, it has a big Asian influence, run-down buildings once owned by Corps, and is home to immigrants of several ethnicities. V has an apartment in one of the megabuildings in the area, and Victor's workshop is in the district, as well, behind Misty's.



>all food plant_

The All Food plant is a pig slaughterhouse located in Night City owned by the All Food Inc. In 2077 the plant is used as a headquarter by the Maelstrom gang led by Royce.
